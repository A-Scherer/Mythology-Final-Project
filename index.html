This is a website that will show you some of the many differences between Ancient Greek and Modern Heroes.

Heroes have always been part of human culture. These stories of strong, often magical people consistently appear in societies and serve as easy ways to teach the children of that society the mortals that their world lives by.

Even to this day, many people still are fascinated with the Ancient Greek heroes, but how did these heroes compare to our modern ones? How did the origins of a hero change over time? What conclusions can be drawn about the respective societies that the heroes came from?
