This is the Ancient Heroes

From Odysseus to Achilles to Perseus, ancient Greek heroes were an abundance in stories. Many villages had smaller tales of a hero that came there once. Other tales were more widely spread and recognized.

Origins:

Ancient Greek Herooes have a very differnet origin story than most of our modern heroes do.

For one, Ancient Greek heroes were almost never considered as a hero until they had died. These mortals had to transcend their morality in order to become a hero.

Ancient heroes were worshipped for their strengths. Homer tells the stories of both Achilles and Odysseus in his pieces The Iliad and The Odyssey. In both of these stories, the man hero demonstrated thewir strength through either victory in battle for Achilles or making a grand journey for Odysseus.

These heroes could also be extremly brutal, as being a person with strong morals was not a requirement in Ancient Greece.
